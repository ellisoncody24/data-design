Conceptual Model


Entities $ Attributes:


User:

  • usersName
  • userShippingInfo

Item:

  • itemId (Primary Key)
  • itemQuantity

Order:

  • orderId (Primary Key)
  • userShippingInfo (Foreign Key)
  • itemId (Foreign Key)

Relationship

  • One user may have many different orders.
  • Orders may have different items.
