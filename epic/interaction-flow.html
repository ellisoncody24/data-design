User Interaction Flow

Title: Add items to a list that Will Scott would like to buy

Description: Will would like to buy a gaming headset and a headset case.

Name of User and Role: Will Scott, a consumer who likes a way to add items to a specific list to buy items.

Usage Preconditions: Must have Amazon account with account information up-to-date.

Usage Postconditions: Being able to add items to a Buy All List.

Interaction Flow: Adding item to list.

  • Will wants to buy a gaming headset.
  • Will goes onto Amazon.com and clicks the search bar on top of page, than types Gaming Headset and selects the search button.
  • Will browses different headsets that are shown on the page and clicks on the image of JBL Pro noise cancelling headphones with built in microphone.
  • Will than finds the Description button and clicks the button to look at the item details to get a better idea of the item.
  • Will scrolls down to find reviews for item, he clicks the Read All Reviews button, and he reads the reviews from other users.
  • Will scrolls back up the page and finds the Add To Shopping-Cart button.
  • Will clicks the Add To Shopping-Cart.
  • Will also wants to buy a case for headset.
  • From the Shopping-Cart Will uses the search bar on top of page and searches for a JBL Headset Case and clicks search.
  • Will browses different Headset Cases on the page and clicks on the image of Plastic Case For JBL Headphones.
  • Will than clicks on the Description button and reads the descriptions to make sure his JBL headset is compatible for JBL Headset Case.
  • Will continues to scroll down and clicks on the Reviews button and reads reviews to make sure of the case overall protection for the Headset.
  • Will scrolls back up the page and locates the Add To Shopping-Cart button.
  • Will clicks the Add To Shopping-Cart button to add Plastic JBL Headphone Case to Shopping-Cart.
  • Will now can click the Shopping-Cart button to view all items in one list
  • Will reviews all information and selects the Buy Now button and is now able to make one purchase for the multiple items.

Frequency of Use: 1-2 times a weeks for different products.
