User Interaction Flow

Title: Add items to a list that Will Scott would like to buy

Description: Will would like to buy a gaming headset and a headset case.

Name of User and Role: Will Scott, a consumer who likes a way to add items to a specific list to buy items.

Usage Preconditions: Must have Amazon account with account information up-to-date.

Usage Postconditions: Being able to add items to a Buy All List.

Interaction Flow: Adding item to list.

  • Will wants to buy a gaming headset.
  • Will goes onto Amazon.com and searches for a gaming headset.
  • Will browses different headsets that are offered and clicks on JBL Pro noise cancelling headphones with built in microphone.
  • Will than looks at the item details to get a better idea of the item.
  • Will scrolls down to find reviews for item, he reads the reviews from other users.
  • Will finds the Add To Shopping-Cart button.
  • Will clicks the Add To Shopping-Cart.
  • Will also wants to buy a case for headset.
  • From the Shopping-Cart Will uses the search bar to search for a Heaadset Case.
  • Will finds a Plastic Case For JBL Headphones.
  • Will reads the descriptions to make sure his JBL headset is compatible for JBL Headset Case.
  • Will reads reviews to make sure of the case overall protection for the Headset.
  • Will locates the Add To Shopping-Cart. button.
  • Will clicks the Add To Shopping-Cart button to add Plastic JBL Headphone Case toShopping-Cart.
  • Will can now go to his Shopping-Cart.
  • Will is now able to make one purchase for the multiple items.

Frequency of Use: 1-2 times a weeks for different products.
